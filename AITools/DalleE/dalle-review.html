DALL-E 3 Review

DALL-E 3 is not trying to beat Midjourney on artistic quality. Built into ChatGPT Plus with no separate account or subscription required, it succeeds at that goal.

01

Where DALL-E 3 Genuinely Wins

DALL-E 3 follows complex detailed text prompts better than any other image AI. Describe a specific UI layout, a data flow diagram, or a product screenshot and DALL-E 3 produces something usable — where Midjourney might produce something beautiful but wrong. For developers who need infographics, UI mockups and technical diagrams, this prompt fidelity is more valuable than raw artistic quality.

02

Where It Falls Short

Artistic quality and style control trail Midjourney significantly. For brand-quality imagery and creative work where aesthetics are the primary criterion, Midjourney produces noticeably superior results. DALL-E 3 also cannot maintain character consistency across images the way Midjourney v6 can.

03

Final Verdict

DALL-E 3 is the right image AI if you are already paying for ChatGPT Plus and need occasional images for UI work, technical documentation or content. It requires no additional cost or account. For professional creative work where quality is the primary concern, Midjourney is worth the additional subscription.
