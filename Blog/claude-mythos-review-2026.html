What the Leak Revealed About Capabilities

The leaked documentation describes several capability upgrades over Opus 4.6. Extended reasoning: Mythos reportedly runs longer reasoning chains with improved coherence across very long reasoning sequences — addressing a known limitation of Opus 4.6 where reasoning quality degrades on tasks requiring more than 100 sequential reasoning steps. Agentic coding: described as surpassing Claude Code's 80.8% SWE-bench score by a significant margin, with improvements specifically in repository-level code changes and multi-file refactoring. Safety architecture: the documentation emphasises a new approach to harmlessness that reduces over-refusal — a common complaint about Claude models — while maintaining safety standards. No performance numbers were included in the leaked documentation.
