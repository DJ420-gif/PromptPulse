What Changed from Qwen3.5 to Qwen3.6-Plus

Qwen3.6-Plus is Alibaba's third major model in under one week — arriving April 2 2026. The headline upgrade is agentic coding: drastically enhanced across frontend web development, repository-level problem solving and complex multi-step coding tasks. The model sets new state-of-the-art on HLE-Verified — a harder, independently verified version of Humanity's Last Exam with 448 expert-written questions in biology, physics and chemistry where PhD experts reach 65% accuracy. The 256K context window uses context-folding to handle conversations exceeding the window by pruning older tool responses at a threshold — a practical solution to long-context degradation. The stated multimodal goal is full agentic execution: not just seeing images and videos but reasoning about them and taking actions in external tools based on what is observed.

Running Qwen3.6-Plus Inside Claude Code

Your Claude Code workflows now run on Qwen3.6-Plus within your 1000 free daily calls.
