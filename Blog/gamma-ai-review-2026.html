01

Why Gamma AI Has 70 Million Users

Gamma AI generates complete slide decks from a single text prompt in under 60 seconds. The output is not a template with your content filled in — Gamma makes structural and design decisions about what information goes on each slide, what the visual hierarchy should be, and what layout best serves the content. This is a genuinely different approach from Canva where you pick a template and the AI helps you fill it, or Beautiful.ai where AI assists layout decisions within a human-driven workflow. For professionals who need to create investor pitches, client proposals, internal updates and educational content regularly the time saving from Gamma's approach is significant enough to build loyalty at scale. The 70 million user count confirmed by multiple sources in March 2026 represents real retained usage not just signups.

02

AI Animations — The January 2026 Update

Gamma added AI-generated animations to slide content in January 2026 — replacing the previous static image generation with animated visual elements. Available on Ultra and Business plans, animations give presentations a more dynamic and contemporary feel that static competitor tools cannot match. In testing, animation quality is impressive for standard content types like data visualisations, process diagrams and concept explanations. The animations are generated alongside the slide content and do not require manual configuration. Gradient card backgrounds were also added in January 2026 giving significantly more visual variety. The overall design quality of Gamma output has improved meaningfully in the six months since launch — early Gamma presentations had a recognisable homogeneous look; current output is more varied and professional.

03

The Export Problem — The Biggest Limitation

Gamma's most significant practical limitation is PPTX export quality. When you export a Gamma presentation to PowerPoint format the layout shifts significantly and requires 15-30 minutes of cleanup before it is usable in a corporate context. Beautiful.ai exports with near-perfect fidelity. Canva exports reliably. Gamma does not. For presentations that will be delivered live within Gamma or shared as a link this is not a problem — Gamma's native playback is excellent. For presentations that need to be shared as a PowerPoint file for someone else to edit or present from a different computer the export limitation is a real blocker. If your final deliverable must be a PPTX file that another person will use Gamma is the wrong choice regardless of generation quality.

04

Pricing and the 400-Credit Free Tier

Gamma Free provides 400 AI credits which are consumed permanently — there is no monthly reset. Once you use your 400 credits on the free plan they are gone and you must upgrade. This is the most common complaint in recent reviews and a significant difference from tools with monthly credit resets. Gamma Plus at $8 per month provides 400 monthly credits. Gamma Pro at $15 per month provides unlimited AI generation. For anyone planning to use Gamma regularly the $15 Pro plan is the only practical option — the free tier credit exhaustion happens faster than most new users expect. The per-deck credit cost means a single complex presentation can consume more than half the free tier allocation.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is Gamma AI worth it in 2026?
Yes for professionals creating regular presentations who value speed over perfection. The $15/month Pro plan is justified for anyone who creates more than 4-5 presentations monthly. Not worth it if final PPTX export quality matters — exports require significant cleanup.
Is Gamma AI free?
Limited free tier with 400 permanent AI credits — no monthly reset. Once used, upgrade required. Gamma Plus $8/month for 400 monthly credits. Gamma Pro $15/month for unlimited generation. The free tier is sufficient for testing only.
Gamma vs Canva for presentations — which is better?
Gamma for AI-generated presentations from scratch — faster and more intelligent layout decisions. Canva for template-based presentations with reliable PPTX export. Gamma's PPTX export is poor. Canva's PPTX export works reliably.
Does Gamma AI have animations?
Yes — AI Animations launched January 2026 on Ultra and Business plans. Generates animated visual elements alongside slide content without manual configuration. Significantly improves the dynamic feel of presentations vs static competitors.
Can Gamma export to PowerPoint?
Yes but quality is poor — exports suffer significant layout shifts requiring 15-30 minutes of cleanup. If you need a clean PPTX file use Canva or Beautiful.ai instead. Gamma is best for native playback and link sharing.
